I also have an experience to work in fields of automotive R&D and nano-technology. Working in diverse fields has exposed me to different methodologies, challenges, and approaches to problem-solving, thus providing an ability to connect ideas across disciplines, ultimately making me a more well-rounded professional.

In the automotive sector, my experience has been dynamic and captivating. I had gained experience in vehicle engineering, indoor testing and fuel research. It also helped me to explore the use of data science within automotive framework which would help in modelling the parameters involved in deciding the life of clutch system.

Working in this industry allowed me to witness the innovation, technological advancements, and ever-evolving landscape of transportation. From collaborating on projects related to upcoming vehicles, latest technologies, and connected car technologies to addressing challenges in manufacturing, supply chain, and sustainability, I have gained invaluable insights into the complexities of the automotive sector.

My experience in nanotechnology has been intellectually stimulating and awe-inspiring. Exploring the world at the nanoscale, where materials exhibit unique properties and phenomena, has opened doors to a realm of endless possibilities. Working in this field has allowed me to delve into nanomaterial synthesis, characterization, and manipulation.

I have witnessed the transformative impact of nanotechnology across various disciplines, from electronics and energy to medicine and environmental science. Contributing to advancements in nanofabrication techniques, nanosensors, and nanomedicine has been both challenging and rewarding. The rapidly evolving nature of nanotechnology keeps me on the cutting edge of scientific discovery and fuels my enthusiasm for exploring the limitless potential of the nano-world.
